First time here?

You’re exactly where you need and want to be

Whether you’ve never practiced yoga or just never been to our studio — this space is for you. Take a breath, we’ll walk you through.
What

What to expect

A calm space
Our studio is warm, and welcoming. Just presence.
Soft Start Sessions
You don’t need to be flexible. Just open.
Gentle guidance
All teachers are here to support, not to push.
Mats & props provided
You don’t need to bring anything but yourself.
Where

How to get here

Our studio is located in the Porto, Portogal — just a few minutes walk from the city center.

You can take tram lines 7 or 8 and get off at the river. From there, it’s a 3-minute walk.

If you’re coming by car, there’s free street parking available on our place, and surrounding streets — just make sure to check for any local signage.
Code of ethics

Healing House Code of Ethics

  1. Respect – Honor diversity and treat everyone with dignity.
  2. Confidentiality – Keep personal information private.
  3. Integrity – Be professional and transparent.
  4. Informed Consent – Explain services clearly, ask for consent, respect hesitation.
  5. Competence – Commit to ongoing learning.
  6. Client Autonomy – Empower each person’s wellness journey.
  7. Non-Discrimination – Offer services fairly and openly.
  8. Accountability – Take responsibility and invite feedback.
  9. Community Engagement – Support community and sustainability.
  10. Compassion – Lead with empathy and kindness.
