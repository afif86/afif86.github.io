Founded & led by Parina Andreeva

Parina Andreeva is a mama of two, originally from the vibrant city of San Francisco, now calling charming Porto, Portugal her home.
The heartbeat of Healing House began in her home as a small group to support mothers and create a welcoming space for refugees. Since then, the Healing House community has blossomed as an extension of her home into the hearts of many. Read more about Parina's  journey how she has fostered community.
Our Mission
Together we can co-create the feeling of home. At Healing House, we nurture community and a profound sense of belonging through sound healing, reiki, yoga, and a rich array of holistic practices. It is a sanctuary designed for rest and renewal, inviting you to return to your true self.
